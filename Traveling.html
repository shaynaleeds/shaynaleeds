Where to next?

If you come to know me, you will learn that I am a travel enthusiast. Ever since I was young, I have always taken a passion into traveling to different places. The experiences I have gained while traveling out of the country has helped me build cultural awareness and respect for others around me. With every new travel adventure, the more I uncover about the world I live in.

My top four favorite places:

Barcelona, Spain
The biggest cosmopolitan in Spain. Main attraction La Romba, Gothic Quarters.
Denver, Colorado
While in the United States, there is nothing like having the mountains so close to you.
Santorini, Greece
The sunrises and sunsets are beyond unreal and the locals are so friendly.
Kauai, Hawaii
This is the place you go when your looking to escape reality with some of the most scenic beaches and lively greenery.
